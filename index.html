Research Overview

I'm an Assistant Professor at Carnegie Mellon University's Department of Social and Decision Sciences, affiliated with the Human-Computer Interaction Institute. My research explores human cognition, AI-driven persuasion, authoritarianism, political psychology, and the nuances of belief modification.
